How to Play

🎯 Objective

Care for your pet by maintaining their 4 stats: Hunger, Energy, Hygiene, and Happiness. If any stat hits 0, it's Game Over!

💰 Earning Money

Look around the house for Chores (Sparkles, Trash, Dirt). Click on them to clean and earn cash. Complete Global chores (like Windows) in all rooms for a big bonus!

🛒 Spending

Use the Computer in the Living Room to access the Marketplace. Buy food, toys, and education upgrades.

💤 Sleeping

Click the Bed in the Bedroom to sleep. You can only sleep at night (10 PM - 6 AM). Sleeping restores Energy and advances to the next day.
