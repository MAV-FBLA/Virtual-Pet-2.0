# Financial Literacy Expansion Plan: "Virtual Pet 2.0"

## Overview
This plan outlines five key modules to deepen the financial literacy aspects of the game. The goal is to move beyond simple "Earning & Spending" to complex concepts like **Risk Management**, **Debt**, **Recurring Expenses**, and **Investment Strategy**.

---

## 1. The "Adulting" Module: Recurring Expenses
**Concept**: Simulate the pressure of fixed costs.
*   **Mechanic - Daily Bills**:
    *   **Rent**: A fixed sum (e.g., $10) deducted automatically every morning at 8:00 AM.
    *   **Utilities**: A variable sum based on usage (e.g., $1 per shower, $0.50 per light toggle).
